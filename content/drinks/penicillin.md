---

Shake blended scotch, lemon juice, and syrup with ice.  Strain into rocks glass
with single large cube.  Top with Laphroaig and garnish with candied ginger.

## Honey Ginger Syrup

 * 1 cup honey
 * 1 cup water
 * 6 inch piece of ginger, peeled and thinly sliced

Combine honey, ginger, and water in a 2 quart saucepan over high; boil.  Reduce
heaet to medium and simmer 5 minutes.  Chill overnight, then strain.
